Skipg.me is a work-in-progress

Hey family, Joey here. I told yous guys I was gonna build a site to memorialize Pop. I have big plans, but they are going to take a while. I try to develop a little bit each day in hopes that this can eventually be a feature rich, engaging way to remember Pop. I'm not using any templates or site generator services like Wix. This is 100% hand-typed code by me in what very little spare time I have. Instead of paying for a hosting service I just rent a small virtual server for $5 a month and I have to configure it to host this site by myself too which is another layer of complexity, but it keeps it cheap. Oh, and by the way, the finished product will look amazing. This is just a placeholder to let you guys know what's up. I'm not going to spend time making this pretty because the time is better spent making creating my vision. Once everything works like I want it THEN I will go back and make this look top-notch (and YES I chose Red Sox official colors because he was such a fan)!
